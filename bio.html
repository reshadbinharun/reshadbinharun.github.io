Reshad's Humble Biography

Reshad is a simple man with simple needs and wants. Since the early age of 5 years, he hunted for food and gathered nuts for his entire village. Needless to say, the women of his village were very fond of Reshad. One day, Reshad decided that as alpha-male of the village community, Reshad, His Excellency, could not go on with a suitable mate. What would his elders think of him. He therefore, in his infinite wisdom, decided to host the Hunger Games to find a mate.
